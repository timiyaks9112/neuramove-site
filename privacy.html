Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, profile information
  • Work Data: Shift logs, earnings, platforms used, work schedule
  • Financial Data: Expenses, expense categories, receipts, recurring expenses
  • Location Data: Zone names you create (we do NOT track GPS location)
  • Ride Data: Trip details if you choose to import CSV files

Automatically Collected

  • Usage statistics and analytics
  • Device type and browser information
  • Log data (timestamps, errors, feature usage)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the NeuraMove service and analytics features
  • Calculate earnings, expenses, and net income estimates
  • Generate insights, statistics, and recommendations
  • Maintain and improve the Service
  • Communicate with you about the Service
  • Debug issues and provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure infrastructure using industry-standard security practices including HTTPS encryption.

Important: While we implement security measures, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information only in limited circumstances: service providers (Cloudflare for hosting), legal requirements, and to protect safety.

We do NOT share your data with delivery platforms, advertisers, or third-party marketing services.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You can access, edit, and delete your data through the NeuraMove interface at any time. To request complete account deletion, contact support@neuramove.ca. We will delete your data within 30 days of request.

6. Children's Privacy

NeuraMove is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do NOT use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

8. GDPR Rights (European Users)

  • Right to access, rectify, and erase your data
  • Right to restrict processing and data portability
  • Right to object to processing and withdraw consent

Contact support@neuramove.ca to exercise these rights.

9. Contact Us

Email: support@neuramove.ca

Privacy Summary

We collect the work and financial data you provide to give you analytics and insights. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with platforms or advertisers. You can access, edit, and delete your data anytime.
