Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use NeuraMove, you may provide:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, profile information
  • Work Data: Shift logs, earnings, platforms used, work schedule
  • Financial Data: Expenses, expense categories, receipts, recurring expenses
  • Location Data: Zone names and descriptions you create (we do NOT track GPS location)
  • Business Intelligence Data: Offer evaluations, thresholds, zone statistics
  • Ride Data: Trip details if you choose to import CSV files

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage statistics and analytics
  • Device type and browser information
  • Log data (timestamps, errors, feature usage)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the NeuraMove service and analytics features
  • Calculate earnings, expenses, and net income estimates
  • Generate insights, statistics, and recommendations
  • Maintain and improve the Service
  • Communicate with you about the Service
  • Debug issues and provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure infrastructure using industry-standard security practices:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted over HTTPS
  • Authentication: Secure session-based authentication
Important: While we implement security measures, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Cloudflare for hosting and infrastructure
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process
  • Safety: To protect rights, property, or safety of NeuraMove, our users, or others

We do NOT share your data with delivery platforms, advertisers, or third-party marketing services.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Data Access and Portability

You can access your data through the NeuraMove interface at any time.

Data Deletion

You can delete specific records (shifts, expenses, zones) through the interface. To request complete account deletion, contact support@neuramove.ca.

Notifications

You can control push notification preferences in Settings.

Data Correction

You can edit and update your data through the NeuraMove interface.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you request account deletion, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal obligations.

7. International Data Transfers

NeuraMove is hosted on Cloudflare's global infrastructure. Your data may be stored and processed in various countries where Cloudflare operates data centers.

8. Children's Privacy

NeuraMove is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and local storage to maintain your session and user preferences. We do NOT use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

10. GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact support@neuramove.ca

11. CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights

We do NOT sell personal information.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, by in-app notification.

13. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your privacy rights, contact:

Email: support@neuramove.ca

Privacy Summary

We collect the work and financial data you provide to give you analytics and insights. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with platforms or advertisers. You can access, edit, and delete your data. Contact us to exercise your privacy rights.
