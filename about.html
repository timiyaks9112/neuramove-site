About NeuraMove

Empowering independent operators with data-driven intelligence to maximize earnings and optimize performance.

🏢

Who We Are

NeuraMove is a technology platform built by gig workers, for gig workers. We understand the challenges of managing multiple income streams, tracking expenses, and optimizing work schedules because we've lived it. Our team is dedicated to creating intelligent tools that transform how independent operators manage their businesses, turning raw data into actionable insights that drive real results.

🎯

What We Do

NeuraMove provides comprehensive business intelligence for independent operators across all gig platforms. Our platform goes beyond simple tracking to deliver:

  • True Earnings Analysis — See your actual net income after expenses, not just gross revenue
  • Multi-Platform Insights — Compare performance across Uber, DoorDash, Skip the Dishes, Instacart, and more
  • Expense Attribution — Link every cost to specific shifts to understand true profitability
  • Tax Preparation — Automated mileage tracking and organized records for stress-free tax season
  • Performance Optimization — Data-driven recommendations to maximize your hourly earnings
💡

Why NeuraMove

Most gig platforms show you what you earned, but they don't show you what you actually kept after expenses. They don't help you understand which platform pays better for your specific situation, or which shifts were profitable versus which lost money. NeuraMove fills this critical gap.

We believe every independent operator deserves access to the same business intelligence that traditional companies use to optimize their operations. Whether you're working one platform or juggling multiple income streams, NeuraMove gives you a unified view of your business performance.

Your data belongs to you. NeuraMove exists to put that data to work for your benefit — showing you the truth about your earnings, identifying opportunities for improvement, and helping you build a more sustainable, profitable business.

👤

From the Founder

"As a Uride driver, I spent months navigating the gig economy without truly understanding what I was earning or how to effectively manage my time. I found myself constantly switching between platforms — Uber, DoorDash, Skip the Dishes — trying to figure out where and when I could maximize my income. Through trial and error, I began recognizing patterns in demand, peak hours, and platform performance, but these insights came from experience alone, not from data.

The reality hit me hard when I sat down to calculate my actual earnings: what looked like decent income on paper was significantly less after factoring in gas, maintenance, insurance, and other operating costs. I realized that most platforms don't give you your true data — they show gross earnings without the full picture of profitability.

That's why I built NeuraMove. Every driver, delivery operator, and gig worker deserves to be empowered with their data. NeuraMove is that platform — built from real experience in the driver's seat, designed to give every operator the insights they need to build a sustainable, profitable business."

Founder & CEO

Former Uride Driver, Multi-Platform Operator
