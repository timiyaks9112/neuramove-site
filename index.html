Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NeuraMove

Is NeuraMove really free?

Yes! NeuraMove is completely free during the beta period. When we do launch paid features, the core tracking and analytics will always have a free tier.

Does NeuraMove automatically import my earnings?

Currently, you log your shifts manually (takes 30 seconds). We're working on platform integrations for automatic import.

How accurate are the tax calculations?

Our mileage deductions use current CRA rates ($0.72/km for first 5,000km, $0.64/km after). Always consult with a tax professional for your specific situation.

What happens to my data if I stop using NeuraMove?

Your data is always yours. Export all your data as CSV or JSON at any time. You can permanently delete your account whenever you want.

Will there be a mobile app?

Yes! We're building native iOS and Android apps with GPS tracking, automatic mileage logging, and platform integrations.
